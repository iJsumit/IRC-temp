Student Exchange Programs

Jaipuria Institute of Management offers robust student exchange programs with prestigious international institutions. We aim to provide cross-cultural exposure and a truly global academic perspective. Students get the opportunity to study a trimester abroad with complete credit transfer.

  • Partnering with Rennes School of Business (France)
  • Collaborations in Malaysia, New Zealand & more.
  • Study under international faculty and build a global network.

Global Immersion Programs

Our Global Immersion Programs go beyond traditional classroom learning. Students are taken on international study tours to experience global business environments, operations, and cultural nuances firsthand.

  • Destinations include Singapore, Malaysia, UAE, Sri Lanka, and Europe.
  • Guided industry visits to global manufacturing hubs and MNCs.
  • Cross-cultural academic sessions and workshops.

Faculty Exchange Programs

To ensure our pedagogy remains globally relevant, our faculty members actively participate in exchange programs and collaborative research with renowned international universities.

  • Exchanges with TAR University (Malaysia)
  • Collaborations with Weltec & Whitireia (New Zealand)
  • Joint research projects and global case study development.

Global Guest Sessions

We bring the world to our classrooms. Throughout the academic year, we host interactive webinars, masterclasses, and guest lectures by international industry experts and academicians.

  • Insights into global market trends and international business strategies.
  • Live Q&A sessions with leaders from multinational companies.
  • High-impact networking opportunities.
